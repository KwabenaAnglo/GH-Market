How We Ship Your Orders

  1. 1 Order Confirmation: You’ll receive an email and SMS confirming your order details.
  2. 2 Processing & Packing: Sellers prepare your items for safe delivery, usually within 24 hours.
  3. 3 Dispatch & Tracking: Your package is handed to a trusted courier. You’ll get a tracking link by SMS or email.
  4. 4 Delivery: Most orders arrive within 1–3 business days in major cities, and 3–5 days nationwide.
  5. 5 Delivery Confirmation: Confirm receipt by signing or entering a code with the courier.
  6. 6 Support: If you have issues with delivery, contact our support team for help.

Shipping FAQs

  • How much does shipping cost? Shipping fees vary by seller and location. Costs are shown at checkout.
  • Can I track my order? Yes! You’ll receive a tracking link once your order is dispatched.
  • Do you deliver nationwide? Yes, we deliver to all regions of Ghana.
  • What if my package is late? Contact support and we’ll help resolve it quickly.
