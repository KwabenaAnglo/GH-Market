Step-by-Step Seller Guidelines

  1. 1 Register as a Seller: Create your account and complete your seller profile with accurate information.
  2. 2 List Your Products: Upload clear photos, write detailed descriptions, and set competitive prices.
  3. 3 Manage Inventory: Keep your stock updated and remove unavailable products promptly.
  4. 4 Process Orders Quickly: Confirm orders, pack items securely, and ship within the promised timeframe.
  5. 5 Communicate Professionally: Respond promptly and politely to buyer messages and questions.
  6. 6 Handle Returns & Issues: Follow marketplace policies for returns, refunds, and dispute resolution.
  7. 7 Maintain High Ratings: Deliver quality products and service to earn positive feedback and build trust.
  8. 8 Stay Updated: Regularly review marketplace announcements and updates for best practices.
  9. 9 Comply with Policies: Follow all marketplace rules, including prohibited items and fair trading standards.
  10. 10 Reach Out for Help: Contact Seller Support if you need assistance at any step.
Thank you for reviewing our Seller Guidelines!
