Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I place an order?
    Browse products, add to cart, and follow checkout instructions. You’ll receive an order confirmation by email.
  • What payment methods are accepted?
    We accept mobile money, debit/credit cards, and cash on delivery in select locations.
  • How do I track my order?
    You’ll get a tracking link by SMS or email after your order is dispatched.
  • Can I return a product?
    Yes, returns are accepted within 7 days of delivery. See our Returns Policy for details.
  • How do I contact support?
    Use the Contact Us page or email support@ghmarketplace.com. Our team responds within 24 hours.
