How We Use Cookies

This Cookie Policy explains how GH Marketplace uses cookies and similar technologies to improve your experience on our website.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit our site. They help us remember your preferences, analyze site traffic, and enhance security.

Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential Cookies: Enable core site functionality (e.g., login, cart).
  • Performance Cookies: Help us understand how visitors use the site.
  • Functional Cookies: Remember your preferences and settings.
  • Marketing Cookies: Personalize offers and measure ad effectiveness.

Managing Your Cookies

  • You can accept or decline cookies in your browser settings.
  • Disabling cookies may affect some site features.
  • We respect your privacy and only use cookies as described here.

Policy Updates

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted here and are effective immediately.
