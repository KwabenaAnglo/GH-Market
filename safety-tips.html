Safety Tips

Stay Safe While Buying and Selling on GH Marketplace

Buyer Safety

Protect yourself when making purchases and ensure you get what you pay for.

Before You Buy

  • Always meet in public, well-lit locations
  • Inspect items thoroughly before payment
  • Ask for detailed photos and descriptions
  • Verify seller's contact information
  • Check seller's ratings and reviews
  • Use secure payment methods only

Payment Safety

Use secure payment methods and avoid common scams.

Secure Payments

  • Use Mobile Money or bank transfers
  • Verify payment confirmations
  • Never share PIN codes or passwords
  • Avoid wire transfers to unknown accounts
  • Keep payment receipts and records

Red Flags to Watch For

Too Good to Be True

Extremely low prices or unbelievable deals are often scams. If it seems too good to be true, it probably is.

Refuses to Meet

Sellers who refuse to meet in person or only want to ship items may be hiding something.

Payment Pressure

Anyone pressuring you to pay quickly or using unusual payment methods should be avoided.

Won't Call or Video

Legitimate sellers should be willing to call or video chat to verify their identity.

Vague Location

Sellers who won't provide a specific meeting location or keep changing it are suspicious.

Rush to Complete

Scammers often create urgency to prevent you from thinking clearly or doing research.

Safety Checklist

Before Meeting

  • Research the item thoroughly
  • Check seller's profile and reviews
  • Agree on meeting location and time
  • Tell a friend or family member
  • Charge your phone fully
  • Plan your route and parking

During Meeting

  • Meet in a public, well-lit area
  • Inspect the item carefully
  • Test electronics if applicable
  • Ask questions about the item
  • Use secure payment methods
  • Get a receipt or proof of purchase

After Transaction

  • Keep all transaction records
  • Leave honest feedback
  • Report any issues immediately
  • Block suspicious users
  • Update your security settings
  • Share your experience with others
