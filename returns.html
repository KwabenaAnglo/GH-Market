Hassle-Free Returns

  1. 1 Check Eligibility: Returns are accepted within 7 days of delivery for most products. Items must be unused and in original packaging.
  2. 2 Request a Return: Contact our support team or use the Help Center to start your return request. Provide your order number and reason.
  3. 3 Prepare the Item: Pack the product securely with all accessories, manuals, and proof of purchase.
  4. 4 Arrange Pickup or Drop-off: Our courier will collect the item or you can drop it at a designated location.
  5. 5 Inspection & Approval: Once received, we inspect the item. Approved returns are processed within 3 business days.
  6. 6 Refund or Replacement: Choose a refund to your original payment method or a replacement product.

Returns FAQs

  • What items can't be returned? Perishable goods, personal care items, and digital products are not eligible unless faulty.
  • How long does a refund take? Refunds are processed within 3 business days after approval.
  • Who pays for return shipping? If the item is faulty or wrong, we cover shipping. Otherwise, the buyer may be responsible.
